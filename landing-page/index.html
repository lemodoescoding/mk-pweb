About Benteng Kedung Cowek

Benteng Kedung Cowek is a historic fortress built during the Dutch colonial period in Surabaya. Originally used as an ammunition storage, it now stands as a well-preserved cultural heritage site. Visitors can explore its walls, gates, and old structures while learning about the local history.

Uniqueness

  • One of the few remaining Dutch fortresses in East Java
  • Architectural features of 17th-century military construction
  • Offers a scenic view of the surrounding Surabaya coastline
  • Hosts occasional cultural events and guided tours
